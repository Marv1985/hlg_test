Liberation
Brewing Co.

Liberation Group approached us to assist them in re-designing their beer and ale offering. Each beer had to have a unique and distinct style, to help set them apart from the competition and to fit into the ever-developing craft beer industry.

We created a series of designs for each beer, some being redesigns and some needing brand new designs creating from scratch. All of the designs reflect the backstory of each beer and all have their own unique, memorable style.
